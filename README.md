# SonicWave
🎵 Elevate Your Acoustic Environment with SonicWave

Tired of operating system mixers altering your sound? SonicWave is a professional 32-band parametric equalizer and real-time DSP (Digital Signal Processing) host designed to output bit-perfect audio directly to your hardware. 

Engineered with a 64-bit floating-point internal processing pipeline, SonicWave introduces absolute zero phase distortion.

🔥 Key Features:
- ASIO & WASAPI Exclusive Mode: Completely bypass OS-level audio mixers to deliver uncompressed, bit-perfect audio directly to your external DAC with <2ms latency.
- 32-Band Visual Parametric EQ: Surgical control over the audio spectrum with adjustable Q-factors and real-time spectral feedback.
- Deep Sub-Bass Synthesis: Proprietary sub-bass algorithms that generate heavy, clean frequencies below 60Hz without muddying mid-tones.
- VST3 / AU Plugin Hosting: Load studio-grade third-party limiters or compressors directly into your system's system-wide playback chain.
- Lossless Native Decoding: Bit-perfect playback of FLAC, ALAC, WAV, and DSD256 up to 384kHz.
